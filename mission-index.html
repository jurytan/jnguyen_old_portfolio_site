Missions

So, you want to feel like a spy, eh? Want to know what a taste of the hacker world is like? Well, here's your chance!


The following are a series of missions that will range in difficulty. Your mission, should you choose to accept this, is to crack these missions and...err, save the princess (who is in another castle at the moment). The first few missions will be pretty easy for any seasoned fellow, but as you progress, you will find that the missions get harder and more difficult than the previous ones. Take your time and use your hint wisely. It could mean the difference between getting the girl and being stuck staring at the password field, rueing the day you chose to cross paths with it.

NOTE: Please keep in mind these exercises are for fun and educational purposes, and should not be used for any malintent or illegal activities. If you haven't noticed, this is extremely influenced by Hack This Site, so if you liked what you saw here, give them a try! Also, there might be bugs with the missions, as I'm building them out by myself and I haven't bulletproofed them (like Hack This Site), so if you notice anything that might ruin the fun (i.e. game-breaker, non-intended outcomes, etc.), please let me know so I can try to fix it.

Plus, it wouldn't be fun if you beated the game without actually solving the puzzle now, would it? :)
